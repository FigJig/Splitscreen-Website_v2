What is Splitscreen? 

Splitscreen is a series of documentaries about local independent developers and people involved in the games scene in Melbourne, Australia. We are looking to give a voice to people who haven’t yet had the platform to do so, to talk about their work and the current games scenes in and around Melbourne.

They are free to watch on YouTube and with permission, to be screened in public spaces.
